FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Which certificates does BUD-INVEST hold?

We hold EN ISO 3834-2:2021 certification for welding quality requirements and EN 1090-1:2009+A1:2011 certification for execution of steel structures. These approvals enable us to deliver projects for demanding chemical, energy and offshore sectors.

How long does a typical project take?

Lead time depends on scope. Simple steel elements usually take 2 to 4 weeks, complex load-bearing structures 6 to 12 weeks, and complete pipeline installations 3 to 6 months. Once we receive technical documentation, we provide a precise schedule within 48 hours.

Do you execute projects in Germany?

Yes. We have been active on the German market for more than 10 years. We work as a general contractor or subcontractor for German companies in the chemical, energy and shipbuilding sectors, including transport and on-site assembly.

Which welding technologies do you use?

We use MIG/MAG, TIG and submerged arc welding (SAW). Our welders hold European qualifications and work with structural steels, stainless steels, acid-resistant steels and specialty alloys.

How does the quotation process work?

Send your technical documentation, drawings or specifications to kontakt@bud-invest.com or via the contact form. Our engineering team will prepare a detailed quotation and schedule within 48 business hours. The quotation is free of charge.
