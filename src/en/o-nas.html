Facilities & Team

Metal Experts

The strength of Budinvest Steel lies in our people. Our team consists of highly qualified engineers, technologists and certified welders with qualifications for advanced welding technologies such as TIG, MIG and MAG.

We operate a modern production hall and a fully equipped welding workshop, allowing comprehensive prefabrication of steel components. We control every stage of the process, from design and cutting to corrosion protection and final on-site assembly.
